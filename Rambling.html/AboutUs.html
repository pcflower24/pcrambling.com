Who Are We?

We are a small charity group that brings people together by taking walks in the country. We were formed in 2015 and we have about 270 members. We have all ages in the club and the majority are in the youthful age. We organize about 200 walks in Accra per annum. We look forward to you joining us.

Our Mission

We are a charity group whose aim is to make people enjoy the the freedom and health benefits that come from being outdoors by waking. We are a family who come together on weekends and on holidays to enjoy walking, socializing and also giving back to the community.
