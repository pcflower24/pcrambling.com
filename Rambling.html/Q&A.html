FAQ

Everyone is welcome to join us on a few walks, atleast two walks is enough for you to decide if you would like to join the club

Check the book online page to get a brief descripton about the grades of walk and to find the walk that is suitable for.

Everyone is welcome on our walks, we always want to see older people walk so we'd love to see you! So quickly hit the join button and register with us.

The lenght of the walk depends on the grade of the walk, grade 'A' walk could last about 4km, grade 'B' could last about 22km and finally grade 'C' could last about 41km of walk

If you are not yet a member you do not have to worry about booking online, you just need to get more information on the website and show up for our next walk. if you are already a member, you just need to turn up at the designated meeting point.

You can wear any comfortable clothes and please dress according to the weather.

The duration of walks depends on the grade of walk, lenght, weather and difficulty of the walk, check the book online page for more info.
